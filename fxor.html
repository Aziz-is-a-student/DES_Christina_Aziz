What is the XOR function?

The exclusive OR (⊕) outputs 1 when the two input bits differ, otherwise outputs 0.


XOR itself is a linear operation, but when combined with nonlinear S-boxes and permutations, it creates the necessary balance between simplicity (for reversibility and speed) and cryptographic strength (via nonlinearity).
